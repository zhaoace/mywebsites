How can we help with our auto industry research?

  • The guideline of our auto research team is to provide problem-oriented solutions.
  • By effectively integrating research methods, data analysis and our marketing knowledge, we can provide you with feasible marketing programs that have strong theoretical basis and can be executed immediately.
  • Guided by the concept of problem-oriented solutions, we provide accurate information that helps you reduce risk by making sound judgments.

What are the features of our auto industry research?

  • We offer clients with a full range of research and consultancy service across such 5 stages as new model development, new model launch, consumer choice, purchase and after-sale service, and helps develop strong brand strategies and effective implementation plans.
  • Development of new car model: concept testing, model testing, price testing, appearance testing, performance testing
  • Launch of new model: market positioning research, communication strategy research, pricing strategy research, competitive research
  • Consumer choice: consumer behavior and attitudes, brand image, communication effectiveness evaluation, consumer characteristics
  • Consumer purchase: dealer satisfaction, diagnosis of effectiveness of dealers’ promotional campaigns.
  • After-sale service: new car user research, consumer satisfaction research.

Who have been benefited from our research service in auto industry?

Typical research projects we have conducted in auto industry:

  • Mystery Customer of a car dealer
  • Consumer satisfaction and demand research on a car company
  • Corporate parent brand and sub-brand architecture research on a car company
  • After-sale service system research on a car company
  • Research on high-end customers’ demand for value-added services of a car company
  • Research on service enhancement strategy of a car company (finance, second-hand car, insurance, leasing)
  • Research on the management and operation of a car company’s member club
  • Study on the demand of potential consumers for family sedans
