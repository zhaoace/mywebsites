How can we help with our luxury industry research?

  • We help you understand high-end market by analyzing the psychology, life style and consumption of up-market consumers. We also explore consumers’ individual demands, and enhance advertising effectiveness of mainstream media in promoting high-end brands.
  • Our research covers the following areas: Industry analysis, competitive research, industry consumption structure research, channel analysis, brand image, consumer attitude, consumer behavior, consumer satisfaction, product testing, media usage and life style.

What are the features of our luxury industry research?

  • With expertise in luxury brand management and marketing, our luxury industry research team helps luxury companies better understand demand of the wealthy class for luxury goods. We also provide strategic support to luxury brands, media industry and advertising companies.
  • Our luxury goods research covers a wide range of high-end brands of watches, jewelry, garment, perfume, luggage bags, and shoes.

Who have been benefited from our research service in luxury industry?

Typical research projects we have conducted in luxury industry:

  • Purchase decision-making research on a high-end men’s watch
  • Consumer demand research on high-end men’s watches
  • Concept testing of a perfume product
  • Package testing of a diamond necklace
  • Life-style and value research on up-market consumer
  • ...
