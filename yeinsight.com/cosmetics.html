How can we help with our cosmetic industry research?

  • What are the characteristics of consumption in cosmetics market? What are the implications for your marketing campaign? We provide in-depth industry insight research covering market observatory and competition research, market entry assessment, market segmentation and positioning.
  • How should a brand market its products according to an effective segmentation of its cosmetic consumers？ We provide in-depth consumer insight research, including Usage and Attitude (U&A) and research on consumers’ values and life styles.
  • What are consumers’ demands for specific products? How to meet these demands? We provide product innovation research in the following areas: benefits of products/service, product positioning, products/service concept testing, package testing, product name/icon/slogan testing, flavor testing, product placement testing, product/service improvement and upgrade
  • How to survive and thrive in the fierce market competition? We provide marketing planning research, which covers pricing research for new product, price adjustment research, copy test, celebrity endorsement test, advertising pre-testing, advertising effectiveness study, promotion/ campaign effectiveness study, research on crisis communication.
  • How to build consumer loyalty to a brand? We provide branding research including monitoring of brand health index, image research, core value and positioning, brand stretching, logo testing

What are the features of our cosmetics industry research?

  • Our experts have rich experience in cosmetic research and marketing consultancy covering a wide range of products.
  • Our research covers the whole life-cycle. No matter which stage your brand or product is at, we will provide you with the most customized research tools.
