How can we help with our FMCG industry research?

  • In-depth Industry Insight Research: market observatory and competitive research, market entry assessment, market segmentation and positioning
  • In-depth consumer insight research: Usage and Attitude (U&A) Research, consumers’ values and life styles
  • Product innovation research: benefits of products/service, product positioning, products/service concept testing, package testing, product name/icon/slogan testing, flavor testing, product placement testing, product/service improvement and upgrade
  • Marketing planning research: pricing research for new product, price adjustment research, copy test, celebrity endorsement test, advertising pre-testing, advertising effectiveness study, promotion/ campaign effectiveness study, research on crisis communication
  • Branding research: brand health index, image, core value and positioning, brand extension, logo testing

What are the features of our FMCG industry research?

  • Our experts have nearly 10 years of experience in FMCG research and marketing consultancy, covering a wide range of products, such as food, beverage, home and personal care.
  • We gain reputation from our in-depth data analysis and constructive advices for developing competitiveness. We have helped many FMCG companies develop new product concepts and increase brand value.
  • Our research covers the whole life-cycle. No matter which stage your brand or product is at, we will provide you with the most customized research tools.

  • Brand tracking in beverage industry (2010-2013)
